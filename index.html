About CCA Church Eldoret Area

Our Story

CCA Church Eldoret Area was founded in **** with a small group of believers led by Archbishop Dr. Mathews Abednego Ajuoga with one vision – to create a Christ-centered community that welcomes all. Over the years, God has grown us into a vibrant church family of worshippers, servant leaders, and missionaries reaching beyond the walls of our sanctuary.

Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by teaching God’s Word, worshipping together, and serving our community.

Vision

To be a church that transforms lives, strengthens families, and impacts the world with the love of Christ.

Core Values

  • Faith — God’s Word is our foundation.
  • Love — Compassion for people.
  • Family — Nurturing relationships and community.
  • Service — Reaching out to meet real needs.
  • Mission — Sharing the gospel locally and globally.
